What is an
AI Fellowship?

The Ascendance Foundry Fellowship is a structured program designed to bridge the gap between academic learning and real-world AI application. We select top-tier university students and pair them with your existing employees.

Together, they undergo rigorous AI training, ensuring your team upskills while delivering tangible projects that drive business value. It's not just an internship; it's a strategic implementation engine.
